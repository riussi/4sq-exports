1. https://foursquare.com/oauth2/authenticate
    ?client_id=YOUR_CLIENT_ID
    &response_type=code
    &redirect_uri=YOUR_REGISTERED_REDIRECT_URI

2. redirect to https://YOUR_REGISTERED_REDIRECT_URI/?code=CODE

3. https://foursquare.com/oauth2/access_token
    ?client_id=YOUR_CLIENT_ID
    &client_secret=YOUR_CLIENT_SECRET
    &grant_type=authorization_code
    &redirect_uri=YOUR_REGISTERED_REDIRECT_URI
    &code=CODE


4. curl https://api.foursquare.com/v2/users/self/checkins?oauth_token=ACCESS_TOKEN&v=YYYYMMDD
